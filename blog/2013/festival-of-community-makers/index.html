Festival of Community Makers

September 5, 2013 · One min read

HackRVA participated in the "Festival of Community Makers" in Fredericksburg, VA.  We presented our X-Box controller controlled power wheels which was a big hit with kids and adults alike. Also, Mark Fraunfelder gave a talk on maker communities and signed autographs of his new book "Made By Hand". It was a great day of learning about other makers and sharing projects.
